Public facts voters should know

Sales taxes could reach 10.875%

Measure B would increase the county sales tax by 0.625%. Based on current CDTFA rate tables, that would raise the total sales tax to 10.875% in some Contra Costa cities.

Another sales tax could appear in November

A regional transit funding measure authorized by state law could place a 0.5% sales tax proposal on the November 2026 ballot in Contra Costa County.

Supervisors proceeded after revised projections

Published reports on March 3 indicated the projected federal funding loss estimate had been revised downward before the Board of Supervisors voted to place Measure B on the ballot.
